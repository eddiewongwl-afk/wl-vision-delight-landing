FAQ

Common Questions

Have more questions? Reach out to our friendly team — we're always happy to help you find the right supply solution for your needs.

What types of products do you distribute?
We specialise in chilled (0°C–4°C) and frozen (-18°C) FMCG products including dairy, fresh produce, seafood, meats, poultry, frozen meals, ice cream, and beverages. We cater to a wide range of temperature-sensitive goods.
Do you serve both businesses and individual customers?
Yes! We serve both B2B clients (supermarkets, restaurants, hotels, wholesalers) and B2C customers (individual buyers and small households). Contact us to discuss the right package for your needs.
What areas do you deliver to?
We currently operate across key regions in Malaysia. Please contact our team for specific delivery route availability and coverage in your area.
Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies by product category and order type. We offer flexible arrangements for both bulk trade orders and smaller consumer purchases. Get in touch to discuss your requirements.
How do you ensure cold chain integrity during delivery?
Our refrigerated fleet maintains strict temperature control throughout transit. We use temperature logging and monitoring systems to ensure products arrive within the required temperature range, every time.
